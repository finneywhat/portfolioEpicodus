About Me

Background
B.S. Business Management, Virginia Tech 2004

I've worked in a variety of roles within the water utility industry since the summer of 2000. After five summers of installing water mains, fire hydrants, and water meters for the City of Fairfax (VA), Water Department, I was hired full-time as a Laborer upon gradution from Virginia Tech. Within two years, I was promoted to a construction administration position where I was responsible for reviewing site plan submissions, managing construction projects, and working with the department's consultants. In addtion, every day, I provided assistance to contractors, other departments, and customers in need of engineering assistance.
After nearly eight years working as a construction administrator, the city's water department was sold to Fairfax Water - one of the country's largest water authorities. My initial role with Fairfax Water was a construction technician, supporting the Construction Engineering Manager in a variety of capacities, from project managmement to field inspections. For the last year and a half of my time with Fairfax Water, I worked as a planning technician within the Short Range Planning division reviewing site plan submissions and running computer models of the water system.
In January 2016, my wife and I sold our home and drove across the country on a journey of self-discovery. We settled in Portland, OR. I decided last year that I wanted to pursue a career as a developer and was intrigued by what Epicodus had to offer.
